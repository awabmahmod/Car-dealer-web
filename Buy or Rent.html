Electric cars are less polluting to the environment

Certainly, electric cars are significantly less polluting to the environment compared to cars that run on burning fuels such as gasoline, diesel, etc., but not by 100%.

Electric cars do not require much expense

One of the advantages of electric cars is that many countries subsidize electric car owners financially, and in some countries electric cars are allowed to park for free, and they are allowed to be charged for free or at a very low cost.

Electric cars contain more modern technology

The electric car gets updates constantly, and it also has a phone application that enables you to know the charging level, start and stop the car remotely and much more without the need to examine it closely, in addition to that you can enjoy video games inside the car as if a real PlayStation was attached to it from the inside.
