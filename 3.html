Description

Featured by dual disk brakes, full size motorcycle frame, cruise control and 72V45Ah high capacity Gel Lead-Acid battery, the Zone GTS is the top-end product on the market and is also one of the most popular electric motorcycles of Emmo. Frame and Suspension: By combining the sporty design of sports motorcycles with our expertise in E-Bikes, you will be able to experience the feeling of riding a full-size motorcycle. The front and rear suspensions have been rigorously designed and tested to ensure its competence to cope with different road conditions. Battery and Motor: Equipped with Emmo 500W GTS Super Torque motor, you will be surprised by its performance in climbing hills and dealing with different road conditions. The upgraded 72V45Ah battery pack can give you a travel range of up to 80 km on a single charge! Plus, a built-in Bluetooth radio system brings more fun to you ride. Reliable Stopping Technology: Hydraulic rear disk brake and dual disk hydraulic front brake are equipped in Zone GTS to ensure a safer stop when needed.. The integrated brake sensor will also cut off the power when you apply the brake. Display and Lights: The backlighted LCD display makes it possible for you to read the speed, battery strength and trip distance when you travel. The LED headlights, tail lights and signal lights are brighter and more energy-efficient.
